Check-In Options

Check-In Time: 03:00 PM

We always recommend in-person check-in, as it gives us the chance to welcome you properly, offer a short tour of the apartment, answer any questions you might have about your stay, and make sure everything is clear and comfortable from the very beginning.
However, if you prefer a self check-in, just let us know in advance.
In that case, the key will be waiting for you on the door. Watch a short video on how to reach the apartments with step-by-step instructions from the main road.

Check-Out Information

Check-out time is by 12:00 PM (noon) at the latest. If you plan to leave earlier, please let us know, this helps us prepare the apartment in time for the next guest.

  • We kindly ask that you report any damage during your stay, so we can address it promptly and ensure everything remains in excellent condition for all guests.
  • Leave the windows slightly open, so the apartment can air out naturally.
  • Take all your personal belongings with you.
  • As a small favor, we kindly ask you to dispose of any trash before you leave. Bins location

    ♻️ Blue bin – for recycling (paper, plastic, glass, etc.)
    🗑️ Green bin – for general waste (non-recyclable items)

  • Please leave the key on the outside of the door.
